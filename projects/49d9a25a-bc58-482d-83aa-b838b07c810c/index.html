Architecture

The GPON Framework

OLT — Central Hub

Optical Line Terminal at the central office controls the network

Passive Splitters

Glass-based devices enabling 1:64 or 1:128 fan-out with zero power

ONU/ONT

End-user devices converting optical signals at customer premises

Key Advantage

One fiber from the CO serves 128 customers with zero active electronics in the field, dramatically reducing failure points and maintenance.
