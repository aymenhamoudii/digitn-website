Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Chef Elena Rossi, The Rustic Hearth began with a simple philosophy: food should be grown with care and prepared with respect. We partner directly with over twenty local farms to bring you the freshest seasonal ingredients.

Every dish tells the story of our region's rich agricultural heritage, reimagined through contemporary culinary techniques.

Elena Rossi Executive Chef & Founder
