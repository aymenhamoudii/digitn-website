# Talking Tom Game 🐱

A fun, interactive pet care game where you can pet, play with, and take care of your virtual cat Tom!

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click and interact with different parts of Tom:
   - **Head** - Pet Tom on the head for a happy purr
   - **Belly** - Watch Tom roll over for belly rubs
   - **Paws** - Tom will play with your cursor
   - **Tail** - Watch Tom swish their tail
   - **Chin** - Tom will meow at you
3. Watch Tom's expressions change based on your interactions
4. Keep petting Tom to increase your friendship level!

## Controls

- **Mouse click** - Interact with Tom
- **Mouse hover** - Get Tom's attention

## Features

- 🎮 Multiple interaction points
- 😺 Animated reactions and expressions
- 🔊 Sound effects
- 💫 Smooth animations
- 🐾 Increasing friendship level

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Have fun playing with Tom!
