Our Story

A Passion for Flavor

Founded in 2018 by Chef Elena Marquez, Ember & Olive was born from a simple belief: that exceptional food tells a story. Every dish we create is a conversation between tradition and innovation, where seasonal ingredients take center stage.

Our name reflects our philosophy — the ember representing the ancient art of fire cooking, the olive symbolizing the Mediterranean roots that inspire our flavor palette. Together, they embody the warmth and authenticity that defines every meal we serve.

"We don't just cook. We curate experiences that linger in memory long after the last bite." — Chef Elena Marquez
