The Reality Check

What We're Facing

Students often believe they're invincible or that health care is for "later." Let's dismantle that myth and examine the immediate stakes threatening your academic journey.

The Vulnerability Myth

Young adults aged 18-25 experience health emergencies at the same rate as older adults. Your age doesn't make you immune to accidents, infections, or chronic conditions that emerge without warning.

Financial Devastation

One ER visit can cost $1,200+. Without coverage, a single health crisis can wipe out savings and force students to choose between medical care and semester tuition payments.

Academic Impact

Untreated health issues lead to missed classes, lower grades, and delayed graduation. Medical debt is a leading cause of student dropout—don't let it derail your degree.
