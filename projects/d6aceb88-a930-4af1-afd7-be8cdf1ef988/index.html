Vulnerabilities

Are There Weaknesses?

While nearly invincible, Viltrumites aren't gods. They possess specific physiological limitations.

Equilibrium Disruption

Their flight requires a delicate inner ear balance. Specific, intense sonic frequencies can completely disrupt their equilibrium, causing severe pain and disabling flight.

Extreme Sustained Heat

While they can survive magma, sustained exposure to astronomical temperatures—like the core or surface of a star—will eventually break down their cellular structure.

Overwhelming Force

The most consistent vulnerability is sheer, brute force from beings of equal or greater strength, such as other Viltrumites or genetically engineered monstrosities.

While nearly invincible, Viltrumites aren't gods. Extreme sensory overload, specifically targeting their delicate inner ear for flight equilibrium, and sheer overwhelming force from equally powerful beings are their only real vulnerabilities.
